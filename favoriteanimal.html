The Majestic Lion

Why Lions Are Fascinating

Lions are powerful, fearless and noble creatures known for their strength, courage, and majestic presence.

Where Lions Live

Most lions are found in sub-Saharan Africa. They prefer grasslands and open woodlands for hunting and roaming.

Top 5 Cool Facts About Lions

  • Lions are the only cats that live in groups.
  • A lion's roar can be heard from 5 miles away.
  • Males have a mane, which makes them look larger and more intimidating.
  • They can sleep up to 20 hours a day
  • They are apex predators, with no natural enemies.
